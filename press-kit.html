Mission Statement

Official Mission

To dignify the migrant experience by connecting families in need with communities ready to respond — where help meets hope.

MigrantPortal is a free platform and nonprofit initiative that bridges the gap between migrant families in crisis and the volunteers, churches, and organizations in their communities who are ready to help. Founded in Kansas City, MigrantPortal serves communities across the United States with a simple belief: the distance between a family in need and a neighbor ready to help should never be too far.

Founder

Nirmal Samuel

Nirmal Samuel is the founder of MigrantPortal and a technology professional based in Kansas City, Missouri. With a deep personal understanding of what it means to navigate life as an immigrant — and a lifelong faith commitment to the dignity of every person — Nirmal built MigrantPortal to close the gap between migrant families in crisis and communities ready to respond.

MigrantPortal began as a simple question: why is it so hard for someone who wants to help to find someone who needs help? That question became a platform, and a platform became a movement.

Nirmal leads MigrantPortal Foundation's technology, partnerships, and growth — driven by the conviction that technology, when built with compassion, can restore dignity to the vulnerable and activate the generosity of communities.

Media inquiries: hello@migrantportal.com
