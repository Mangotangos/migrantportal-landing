Our team recently connected with a migrant family of 5 who arrived from Central America in early 2025. The father works part-time in construction and the mother is caring for three children ages 3, 7, and 10. They are currently staying in a crowded room with extended family and urgently need help finding transitional housing or a shelter with family accommodations.

The 10-year-old has not yet been enrolled in school and needs help with the process. The youngest child needs a pediatric wellness checkup. The family speaks Spanish and very limited English — a Spanish-speaking volunteer or interpreter would make a tremendous difference.

Any help with housing referrals, school enrollment support, or donated essentials (bedding, hygiene items, clothing for kids) is deeply appreciated. Thank you for walking alongside this family.
