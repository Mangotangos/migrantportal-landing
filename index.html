How It Works

Three roles. One mission.

Every person in the MigrantPortal community plays a specific role in moving a family from crisis to stability.

🤝
Connector

Submit Needs

ESL teachers, pastors, and social workers who know migrant families personally. They submit specific needs on behalf of families and track outcomes.

ESL Teachers Pastors Social Workers Case Managers
💚
Responder

Meet the Need

Churches, volunteers, and donors who browse the live needs feed, claim what they can fulfill, and provide real, tangible help to families.

Churches Volunteers Donors Professionals
🛡️
Admin

Verify & Protect

Trusted coordinators who review submitted needs, approve verified requests, manage the platform, and ensure migrant families are protected.

Coordinators Org Leaders Network Admins

Features

Everything a compassionate network needs

Built from real needs of real families — every feature exists to move people from crisis to community.

📋

Live Needs Feed

Responders see a real-time feed of verified needs filtered by category, urgency, and location. Claim with one tap.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Profiles

Connectors build rich family profiles — origin country, language, adults, children — so responders can serve with context.

💳

Secure Donations

Responders can donate directly to a specific need through Stripe. 100% of donations are tied to a verified family need.

📅

Volunteer Scheduling

Organizations post volunteer shifts. Responders sign up, track hours, and build a lasting commitment to the community.

📍

Map View

See all active needs plotted on a map. Color-coded by urgency. Tap to view details, claim, or donate from the map.

📊

Impact Dashboard

Public stats showing fulfilled needs, categories served, top cities, and community leaderboard. Proof that it's working.

💬

In-App Chat

Connectors and responders communicate directly within each need thread — no phone numbers shared, full privacy protected.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Matched responders are notified the moment a relevant need is approved. Connectors are updated at every step of fulfillment.

📚

Resource Directory

Community-contributed directory of local services — legal aid, food banks, ESL classes, housing — searchable and categorized.
