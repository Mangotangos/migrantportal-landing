Why We Exist

The gap between need and help shouldn't be this wide.

Every day, migrant families arrive in cities across America — disoriented, exhausted, and often invisible to the systems meant to help them. They need food. A warm coat. A doctor's visit. Someone to translate. Someone to care.

Meanwhile, churches, volunteers, and nonprofits in those same cities have exactly what these families need. Resources. People. Willingness. But no way to connect.

"Where Help Meets Hope."

MigrantPortal was built to close that gap. A migrant family submits a need — in their own language, from their phone. A local responder sees it, clicks "I can help," and shows up. An organization tracks impact and coordinates response at scale.

No bureaucracy. No long waitlists. Just help, meeting hope — in real time.

The Platform

Simple by design. Powerful by community.

1

A family submits a need

Migrants submit needs through the app — food, shelter, medical, legal, translation — in minutes, in their own language.

2

A neighbor responds

Volunteers and partner organizations in the same city see the need and respond. Real people. Real help. Within hours.

3

A community grows stronger

Every fulfilled need is a story of dignity restored. Organizations track impact. Networks grow. Communities change.

Who We Serve

Three communities. One platform.

🧭

Navigators

Migrant families and individuals who need help navigating a new country, city, and system.

🤝

Responders

Compassionate volunteers who want to give their time, resources, or skills to help a real family.

🏛️

Organizations

Churches, nonprofits, and community groups who coordinate response and track collective impact.

🌍

Ethnic Communities

Cultural associations and diaspora networks who serve as bridges between new arrivals and local resources.

⛪

Faith Communities

Churches and faith-based groups answering the call to love their neighbors — practically, not just spiritually.

📣

Advocates & Donors

Individuals and funders who want to see systemic change — one family, one need, one act of kindness at a time.

Our Mission

To dignify the migrant experience by connecting families in need with communities ready to respond — where help meets hope.

MigrantPortal Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization in formation, currently serving communities in Kansas City, Houston, Chicago, Phoenix, and Los Angeles.
